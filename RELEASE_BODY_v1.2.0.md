## DCUO Quality of Life — v1.2.0

A huge update focused on **getting better at DPS**, plus full **Builds** and **Tier List** systems and **group DPS via the in-game Scorecard**.

### ⚔ DPS meter is now a coach
- MY PERFORMANCE strip: sustained DPS, **Burst (best 10s)**, **Activity %** with an S/A/B/C/D/F grade, **Crit %**, **APM**, and a **Might / Supercharge / Precision** split bar.
- **Live Coach** — tells you the #1 thing to fix right now (downtime, low crit, too weapon-heavy, no supercharge) or your power's pro tip.
- **Personal bests** per power ("🏆 NEW BURST RECORD"), Sparring-Target parse mode, **Copy Parse** to clipboard.
- Reports: DPS-over-time graph, **rotation timeline** with downtime markers + ideal-rotation compare, **death recap**.

### 👥 Group DPS — Scorecard OCR (TOS-safe, no memory reading)
- Calibrate the in-game **Scorecard → Leaderboard**, press **F10**, and see every player's **DPS ranked** (damage ÷ time), you highlighted. Reads the screen, exactly like the chat/party-frame OCR.

### 🛠 Builds tab — every power, every role
- All 15 powers with **DPS, the support role (Tank/Heal/Troll), and a Battle version** of each.
- **Dual ability trays (12 abilities)**, **5 artifacts** (with why + alternatives), **best-in-slot iconics** (+ alternatives), recommended **allies**, mods, skill points, rotations and tips.
- Every ability is **tagged** (DMG / SHIELD / HEAL / DEBUFF / GROUP POWER / ST TAUNT / COMBO / SUPERCHARGE / ICONIC …).
- **Power-priority banner**: artifacts ~50-70% of your power, allies ~10-20%, the rest skill points + gear — level your 5 artifacts to 200 first.
- A **tier badge** on every build (incl. battle roles).

### 🏆 Tier List tab
- DPS / Tank / Heal / Troll / Battle Tank / Battle Heal / Battle Troll / Overall — color tiers (S+ → B) with a one-line rationale per power. Reflects the current (June 2026) meta, incl. the **Water rebalance (S+)**.

### 🔧 Fixes / polish
- Overlay is now fully **click-/drag-interactable in windowed mode** (no more auto-hide on click).
- UI now **fills the window** (responsive layouts).

> Self-contained single-file build — no install, just run `DCUO-QualityOfLife.exe`. Reads only your own `combat.log` + screen pixels (no memory/packet interception).
